1 – WHAT IS IPTV? Internet Protocol Television (IPTV) uses Internet technology to send television programming to your TV. IPTV uses a broadband Internet connection for top international programming directly to your home without a satellite dish. Simply connect your receiver to your IPTV Internet from the house and on your TV, then sit back and enjoy.
